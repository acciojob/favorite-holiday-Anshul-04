Maldives - The Tropical Paradise


The Maldives is a beautiful island country located in the Indian Ocean. With its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and exotic marine life, it is the perfect destination for a tropical getaway. The Maldives consists of a chain of 26 atolls and over 1,000 islands, each with its unique charm and beauty.

  1. Scuba Diving and Snorkeling: The Maldives is known for its incredible underwater world, where you can swim alongside manta rays, whale sharks, and colorful schools of fish.
  2. Island Hopping: Explore the various islands and their unique cultures, cuisine, and natural beauty.
  3. Sunset Cruises: Witness the breathtaking sunset over the Indian Ocean while cruising on a traditional Maldivian boat.
  4. Spa and Wellness: The Maldives offers a range of luxurious spa treatments and wellness programs that will leave you feeling rejuvenated and refreshed.
  5. Water Sports: Enjoy a variety of water sports, including jet skiing, parasailing, and windsurfing, in the crystal-clear waters of the Maldives.
  • Hedhikaa: A variety of savory snacks, including fish cakes, samosas, and roshi (flatbread).
  • Mas Huni: A traditional Maldivian breakfast dish made with tuna, coconut, and onions.
  • Fihunu Mas: Grilled fish marinated in spices and served with rice and vegetables.
  • Bis Keemiya: A crispy pastry filled with vegetables or meat.
  • Rihaakuru: A thick fish paste used as a condiment for rice and other dishes.
